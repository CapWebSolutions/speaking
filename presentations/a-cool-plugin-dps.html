Display Posts Shortcode

in 5 Minutes or Less

  • What is it?
    Display Posts Shortcode plugin

    • Display Posts Shortcode was written to allow users to easily display listings of posts without knowing PHP or editing template files.

  • Where to get it?

DPS - 4 Minutes or Less

  • Why use it?

    • Needed to display groups of posts several categories configurations on single page using Beaver Builder

  • Lots of parameters
  • Customizations with filters
  • Extensions on plugin

Plugin Meta

3 minutes or less

  • Who - Bill Erickson
    https://github.com/billerickson/display-posts-shortcode
  • How - Fun Part

Examples - 2 mins or less

[display-posts category="agency,hospital,malpractice-company" 
category_display="false" include_excerpt="true" excerpt_more="..."]

Example - Featured Image w/1 Category

[display-posts image_size="thumbnail" wrapper="div" 
wrapper_class="my-grid-layout alignleft" category="supporting agency" 
category_display="false" include_excerpt="true"]
